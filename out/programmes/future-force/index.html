Bridging the Gap

Developed by Oakleaf Training and Consulting in collaboration with Entreva Hub, FFP identifies and fully develops the hidden potential of young people over two intensive months.

The Future Force Program is a catalyst for the development of young people, providing them with essential tools to be more focused, prepared, and skilled for their future careers and personal lives. We offer lifetime on-going mentoring for participants who graduate from the program.

Purpose

"To engage the minds, hearts, and hands of young people and prepare them for life at their full potential."

Key Objectives

  • Identify and develop student potential for full functionality
  • Develop entrepreneurial skills for the modern economy
  • Instill critical life skills and resilience
  • Equip participants with critical technology and AI skills
